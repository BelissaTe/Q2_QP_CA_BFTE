BACKGROUND

Mental health is one of the components that make our life. This includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. One of the main factors that affect our mental health is academics. Research has found that "increased prevalence of psychological and physical problems like melancholy, anxiety, nervousness, and stress-related disorders‚Äù can be brought on by excessive academic stress, and this can have a negative impact on academic performance. According to the website eric.ed.gov, a study that was conducted in a public national high school in the Mandaue City Division, Cebu, has shown that Mathematics is considered as one of the most difficult subjects in the among Filipino students.

Mathly Mentally Health is the perfect website that aims to help Filipino students with their metal health. It mainly foccuses on academics, specifically the subject Mathematics, as studies had shown that it is one of the most difficult subjects. Mathly Mentally Health offers multiple courses for weekly plans or monthly plans -- either for one week or one month, depending on what plan the user will choose. Each of these plans will contain learning guides in pdf version that students can simply download for their use, as well as supplementary videos originally made by the Mathly Mentally Health members.

INITIATIVES

Mathly Mentally Health tries to execute 5 different learning techniques in learning Mathematics that each stand for the 5 major types of learners. These processes include the Read/Write learners, Visual Learners, Auditory Learners, and Kinesthetic Learners. For example, we have the memorization process for the Read/Write learners, where the website gives you short quizzes after you study the learning guides uploaded for a specific topic or concept. These quizzes usually involve the questions 'What?', 'Who?', and 'Why?'. The next process that we execute is the picture-perfect process. This basically involves diagrams, tables, and graphic organizers. This helps the visual student learner master the needed information in the form of images or visual representations.
